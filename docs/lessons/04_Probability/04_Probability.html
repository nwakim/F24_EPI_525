What is a probability?

Definition 2. The probability of an outcome is the proportion of times the outcome would occur if the random phenomenon could be observed an infinite number of times.

Law of Large Numbers As more observations are collected, the proportion of occurrences with a particular outcome converges to the probability \(p\) of that outcome.
